Spamsilog

Description

A Filipino breakfast dish that consists of garlic rice, eggs, and Spam. "Silog" means egg in Tagalog.

Ingredients

  • 1 can Spam
  • 4 eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 5 cups rice, cooked
  • 2 tsp fish sauce

Steps

  1. Cut Spam into 8 or 9 slices.
  2. Heat two tablespoons oil in a large skillet and fry eggs sunny-side up.
  3. Remove eggs from skillet and add Spam. Fry until slices are golden brown on both sides.
  4. Transfer Spam to a clean plate and add more oil if needed. Add garlic and saute until fragrant.
  5. Add rice and saute further for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add fish sauce and mix to combine for 30 seconds.
  7. Turn off heat.
  8. To assemble, add one egg, 2 slices of Spam, and 1 scoop of garlic rice onto a plate. Serve immediately.
