Caprese Salad

Description

A very simple Italian salad that is made using tomatoes, basil leaves, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • 8-12 oz fresh mozzarella cheese (NOT shredded)
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Sea salt
  • Pepper
  • 6-8 basil leaves

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes into slices half an inch wide. Arrange tomato slices in a single layer on a plate.
  2. Cut mozzarella into thin slices. Place one slice of mozzarella between two slices of tomato.
  3. Drizzle tomatoes and mozzarella with olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper.
  5. Chop basil leaves roughly or tear using fingertips. Spread on top.
  6. Serve immediately.
