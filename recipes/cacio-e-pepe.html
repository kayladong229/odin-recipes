Caprese Salad

Description

A pasta dish made with cheese and pepper. Easy-peasy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb dry spaghetti
  • Salt
  • 4 tbsp salted butter, divided
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated, plus more for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large pan, add a dash of salt to 6 cups of water. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti to pot and cook for two minutes less than indicated on package directions.
  3. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain spaghetti.
  4. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in skillet that pasta was cooked in. Add black pepper and saute until fragrant.
  5. Add 1/2 cup of reserved pasta water and stir until a sauce forms.
  6. Reduce heat to low and add spaghetti, Parmesan, and remaining 2 tablespoons of butter to pan.
  7. Using tongs, quickly toss contents of skillet until sauce becomes creamy. Serve immediately, with additional pepper or Parmesan as garnishes if desired.
